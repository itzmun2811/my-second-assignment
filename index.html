Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

The period of a child's life from 3 to 6 years old is known as preschool.
During this time, children are transitioning from toddlerhood to a more formal learning environment.

Elementary School

An elementary school is a school where children are taught for the first six or
sometimes eight years of their education.

Middle (10-16) Years

Middle school for students aged 10–16 years is often grades 6–10
and is based on the International Baccalaureate Middle Years Programme
